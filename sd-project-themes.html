To open the exciting world of scientific discoveries to a broader audience, we formulate and produce multi-media content through partnership with scientists and researchers,  who often appear on camera as lead presenters of content.

 

Through our Scientist Filmmaker Initiative, an abstruse world of statistics and scientific breakthroughs are transformed into in-depth films featuring clear storylines, stunning visuals and moving sonic elements.

We develop and produce films and media initiatives focused on in-depth documentation of natural world with an emphasis on natural sciences.

 

Analytical presentation of scientific topics, capturing animal behavior, illuminating the life history of a species and documenting recent discoveries featuring an under-reported and unique story-lines are among the common themes of our productions.

We support conservation groups, organizations, individuals and environmental activists’ agendas by producing media that helps leverage their efforts.

 

Our goal is to offer strategic advice as well as production expertise in developing media packages aimed at achieving maximum outreach.
